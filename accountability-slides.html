Why It Matters
  • Consistency — every concern is handled the same way, regardless of who is involved
  • Clarity — issues are documented clearly so there is no ambiguity later
  • Protection — a written record protects both the employee and the company equally
  • Accountability — expectations are set and tracked fairly across the entire team
How It Benefits You
  • Transparency — you always know exactly where you stand and why
  • A fair process — nothing happens behind the scenes; everything is documented
  • Your voice matters — acknowledgment is not agreement; you can always respond
  • Open door — the Sales Director is available if any situation warrants escalation
The goal is not to penalize — it is to create a clear, fair, and documented process that everyone can trust. Accountability works in both directions. When expectations are documented and acknowledged, it creates a workplace where problems get resolved, not ignored.

What This Means for You

Your Responsibilities

Simple steps to stay informed and in good standing.

Signature Requirement
  • All accountability documents require your digital signature to be considered compliant
  • Your signature confirms you have received and read the document — nothing more
  • It is not an admission of guilt or agreement with the content
  • Unsigned documents cannot be used to fulfill compliance requirements on either side
24-Hour Acknowledgment Window
  • Once an accountability document is issued, you have 24 hours to acknowledge receipt
  • You will receive a notification through the Field Manager Pro app
  • Failure to acknowledge within 24 hours may result in a clock-in restriction until completed
  • If you have questions or concerns, reach out to your manager or the Sales Director immediately
Open Door Policy: Should any accountability matter feel unclear, unjust, or require further discussion, the Sales Director maintains an open door for all team members. You are encouraged to use it. Transparency and trust go both ways — we are committed to making sure every situation is handled with fairness and respect.
