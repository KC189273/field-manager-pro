# Appointments & Booking

## How Clients Book
1. Clients access your booking page via your QR code or direct link
2. They sign up with their name and email
3. They select a barber, choose services, pick a date/time
4. The appointment request is sent to the barber for confirmation

## Confirming / Declining Appointments
- New appointments appear on your Barber Dashboard
- Tap an appointment to see details
- Tap "Confirm" to accept or "Decline" to reject
- If declining, you can propose an alternative time
- Unconfirmed appointments expire automatically after 24 hours

## Appointment Status
- **Pending**: Waiting for barber to confirm
- **Confirmed**: Barber accepted, client notified
- **Completed**: Service was delivered
- **Declined**: Barber rejected (with optional reason)
- **Expired**: Not confirmed within 24 hours
- **Cancelled**: Client cancelled

## Appointment Reminders
- Automatic reminders are sent to clients 1 hour before their appointment
- Barbers also receive a reminder notification

## Common Issues
- Not seeing new appointments: Make sure push notifications are enabled
- Client can't find your booking page: Share your QR code or the direct link from Shop Setup
- Wrong time showing: Check your availability settings in your barber profile
