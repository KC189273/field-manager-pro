# Barber Availability

## How do I set my availability?
1. Access your availability settings through your barber profile.
2. For each day of the week, set your **start time** and **end time**.
3. Toggle days off that you don't work.
4. Save.

## How does availability interact with shop hours?
- **Shop hours** (set by the shop owner in Shop Setup) define when the shop is open.
- **Barber availability** defines when YOU are available within those hours.
- Clients see the intersection: they can only book during times when BOTH the shop is open AND you're available.
- Your availability can be more restrictive than shop hours (e.g., shop opens at 9, but you start at 10).

## Who can edit my availability?
- **You (barber)** can set your own availability.
- **Shop owner** can set availability for any barber in their shop.
- Barbers cannot edit each other's availability. The error "Forbidden" appears if you try.

## Why can't clients book certain times?
Check:
1. Your availability for that day — is it set?
2. Do you already have an appointment at that time? (time slot conflict)
3. Is there enough time for the service duration + cleanup time before your next appointment or closing time?

## What are cleanup minutes?
Cleanup minutes (set in your barber profile) add a buffer between appointments. If you set 15 minutes of cleanup time and a haircut takes 30 minutes, the system blocks out 45 minutes total for that slot.
