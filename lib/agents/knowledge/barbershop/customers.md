# Customer Management

## How Customers Sign Up
- Customers create an account through your QR code or booking link
- They enter their name, email, and phone number
- Once signed up, they can book appointments directly

## Viewing Your Customers
- Go to "My Customers" from the nav menu
- See all customers who have booked with you
- View their booking history and any notes

## Customer Notes
- Add private notes on any customer (preferences, hair type, favorite styles)
- Notes are only visible to you, not the customer
- Helpful for remembering preferences between visits

## Common Issues
- Customer not appearing in your list: They need to have booked at least one appointment
- Customer can't sign up: Make sure your shop code is correct and your booking page is active
