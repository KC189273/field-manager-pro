# Customer Management

## How do customers sign up?
Customers create an account by:
1. Scanning your shop's **QR code** or opening your direct booking link.
2. Going through the customer signup page (`/customer-signup?code=XXXX`).
3. Entering their name, email, and phone number.
4. Once signed up, they can book appointments directly.

The shop code connects them to your specific shop.

## How do I view my customers?
Go to **My Customers** from the nav. You'll see all customers who are associated with your shop. Each entry shows their name and contact info.

## Can I add notes on a customer?
Yes. Tap on any customer to view their profile, then add a **private note**. Notes are only visible to barbers and the shop owner — customers cannot see them. Use notes to remember preferences, hair type, favorite styles, allergies, etc.

## Why isn't a customer showing in my list?
Customers appear in your list after they create an account through your shop's signup flow. If someone booked but isn't showing:
- Make sure they signed up using your shop code (from your QR code or link).
- They need to have completed the signup process, not just started it.

## Can I add a customer manually?
No. Customer profiles can only be created through the self-signup flow (via your QR code or booking link). Barbers and shop owners cannot manually add customers. If you need to add someone, share your QR code or booking link with them and have them sign up.
