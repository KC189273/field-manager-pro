# Services

## How do I add a service?
1. Go to **Shop Setup > Services** (if you're the shop owner) or access services through your barber profile settings.
2. Tap **Add Service**.
3. Enter:
   - **Name**: what clients see (e.g., "Haircut", "Fade", "Beard Trim")
   - **Price**: in dollars
   - **Duration**: in minutes
4. Save. The service appears on your booking page immediately if it's active.

## How do I edit or deactivate a service?
Tap on any service to edit its name, price, or duration. Toggle the **Active** switch to deactivate a service — it will be hidden from clients but not deleted.

## Can I reorder services?
Services appear on the booking page in the order they were created. There is currently no drag-and-drop or manual reorder feature in the UI. If you want a specific service to appear first, it needs to be the first one you create.

## Who can manage services?
- **Barbers** can manage their own services only.
- **Shop owners** can manage services for any barber in their shop.
- If a barber tries to edit another barber's services, they'll see: "Forbidden."

## Why can't clients see my services?
Check that:
1. Your services are marked as **active** (not toggled off).
2. You have at least one service added.
3. Your barber profile is **listed** (visible on the booking page).

If you have zero active services, clients can still see your name but won't be able to book because there's nothing to select.

## What fields are required?
A service name is required. The error "Service name required" appears if you try to save without one. Price and duration should also be set for clients to see meaningful information.
