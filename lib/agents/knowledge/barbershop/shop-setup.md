# Shop Setup

## Getting Started
Go to Shop Setup from the nav menu. There are 4 tabs:

### Info Tab
- Enter your shop name, address, and phone number
- This information appears on your booking page

### Services Tab
- Add each service you offer (e.g., Haircut, Fade, Beard Trim)
- Set the price and duration (in minutes) for each service
- Toggle services active/inactive as needed
- Drag to reorder how services appear to clients

### Hours Tab
- Set your shop's operating hours for each day of the week
- You can also set individual barber availability that overrides shop hours

### Payment Tab
- Add your Venmo username or Cash App tag
- Clients see your payment info after their appointment is confirmed

## QR Code
- Your shop's QR code is available in Shop Setup
- Clients scan it to access your booking page
- You can also download it and print it for your shop

## Common Issues
- Clients can't see your services: Make sure services are marked as "active"
- Booking page shows wrong hours: Check both shop hours and individual barber availability
- QR code not working: It links to your download/booking page — make sure you're connected to the internet
