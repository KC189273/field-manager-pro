# Shop Setup

## Who can access Shop Setup?
Only **shop owners**. Barbers and customers cannot access this page.

## How do I set up my shop?
Go to **Shop Setup** from the nav. There are 4 tabs:

### Info Tab
- Enter your shop name, address, and phone number.
- This information appears on your public booking page.

### Services Tab
- Add each service you offer (e.g., Haircut, Fade, Beard Trim, Line Up).
- For each service, set:
  - **Name**: what clients see when booking
  - **Price**: in dollars
  - **Duration**: in minutes (how long the service takes)
- Toggle services **active/inactive** — inactive services don't appear to clients.
- Drag to reorder how services appear on the booking page.

### Hours Tab
- Set your shop's operating hours for each day of the week.
- Toggle days on/off (e.g., closed on Sundays).
- Individual barber availability can override shop hours (set in the barber's profile).

### Payment Tab
- Add your **Venmo username** and/or **Cash App tag**.
- Clients see your payment info after their appointment is confirmed.

## QR Code
Your shop's QR code is available in Shop Setup. When scanned:
1. It opens the app download page with your shop code displayed.
2. New clients download the app and sign up using your shop code.
3. They land on your booking page ready to book.

You can download the QR code image and print it to display in your shop.

## Why can't clients see my services?
Make sure your services are marked as **active** in the Services tab. Inactive services are hidden from the booking page.

## Why does the booking page show wrong hours?
Check both:
1. **Shop hours** in the Hours tab
2. **Individual barber availability** in the barber's profile

Barber availability can override shop hours. If a barber sets different hours, those are what clients see for that barber.
