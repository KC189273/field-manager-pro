# Account Settings

## How do I change my password?
1. Go to **Settings** (gear icon in the nav).
2. Tap **Change Password**.
3. Enter your current password and your new password twice.
4. Tap Save.

Passwords must be at least 6 characters. They are case-sensitive.

## I forgot my password
1. On the login screen, tap **Forgot Password**.
2. Enter your email address.
3. Check your email for a reset link. The link expires after 1 hour.
4. Click the link and enter your new password.

If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder. The email comes from noreply@fieldmanagerpro.app.

## How do I update my profile photo?
1. Go to **Settings**.
2. Tap your current avatar/photo at the top.
3. Select a new photo from your device.
4. The photo uploads and replaces your current avatar across the app.

## How do I log in?
1. Open the app or go to fieldmanagerpro.app.
2. Enter your **username** (not your email).
3. Enter your **password** (case-sensitive).
4. Tap Log In.

## What is my username?
Your username was set when your account was created by your manager. It's different from your email. If you don't know your username, contact your DM or admin.

## First-time login
If this is your first time logging in, your manager gave you a temporary password. After logging in, the app automatically redirects you to the **Change Password** page. You must set a new password before you can use the app. This happens whenever your account has the "must change password" flag set.

## App not loading / blank screen
1. Force-close the app and reopen it.
2. Check your internet connection.
3. Clear your browser cache if using the web version.
4. Try logging out and logging back in.
5. If on mobile, make sure you have the latest version from the App Store or Google Play.
