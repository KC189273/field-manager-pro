# Account Settings

## Changing Your Password
Go to Settings (gear icon) and tap "Change Password". Enter your current password and your new password twice. Passwords must be at least 6 characters.

## Forgot Password
On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password" and enter your email. You'll receive a reset link. The link expires after 1 hour.

## Profile & Avatar
Tap your avatar in the top-right corner or go to Settings. You can upload a profile photo and update your display name.

## Push Notifications
Go to Settings > Notifications to control which notifications you receive. You can toggle email notifications, push notifications, and specific event types (clock events, schedule changes, task reminders, etc.).

## App Not Loading / Blank Screen
1. Force-close the app and reopen it
2. Clear your browser cache if using the web version
3. Check your internet connection
4. Try logging out and logging back in

## Can't Log In
- Make sure you're using the correct username (not email)
- Passwords are case-sensitive
- If you've forgotten your password, use the "Forgot Password" link
- Contact your manager if your account may have been deactivated
