# Getting Started

## Downloading the App
- **iOS**: https://apps.apple.com/app/field-manager-pro/id6762024114
- **Android**: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=app.fieldmanagerpro
- **Web**: https://fieldmanagerpro.app (works in any browser)

## First Login
1. Your manager or admin will create your account and give you a username and temporary password.
2. Open the app and enter your **username** and **password**.
3. You may be prompted to change your password on first login.

## What You'll See on the Dashboard (Wireless Retail)
The dashboard is personalized by your role:
- **Employees**: your current shift status, today's scheduled shift, active tasks, recent notifications.
- **DMs (managers)**: clocked-in employee count, open flags, team size, pending expenses, plus your own shift info.
- **SD/Owner**: org-wide summary — total team, clocked-in count, flags, pending expenses.

## What You'll See (Barbershop)
- **Customers**: the Book page to request appointments.
- **Barbers**: the Barber Dashboard showing incoming and upcoming appointments.
- **Shop Owners**: the Barber Dashboard plus Shop Setup for managing your shop.

## Where is everything?
Tap the **More** menu (three dots or hamburger icon) at the bottom of the screen to see all available features. You can pin your most-used features to the bottom tab bar by long-pressing them in the More menu.

## Setting Up Push Notifications
When you first open the app, you'll be asked to allow push notifications. Say **Yes** — this is how you get alerts for schedule changes, task assignments, appointment requests, and more. You can always adjust specific notification types in Settings.
