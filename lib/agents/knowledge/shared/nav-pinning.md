# Navigation Tab Pinning — "How do I customize my bottom tabs?"

## How to Pin Features to the Tab Bar
1. Tap the **More** button (≡ or ··· icon) at the bottom of the screen.
2. You'll see all available features for your role.
3. **Long-press** on any feature to pin it to your bottom tab bar.
4. Pinned features appear as quick-access tabs at the bottom of every screen.

## Maximum 4 Pins
You can pin up to **4 features** at a time. If you try to pin a 5th, you'll need to unpin one first.

To unpin: long-press the pinned tab and select unpin, or go to the More menu and long-press the feature again.

## Why can't I see [feature] in the More menu?
Each feature is filtered by your role. If a feature doesn't appear in your More menu, your role doesn't have access to it. See the Roles & Permissions doc for what each role can access.

## Pin preferences are saved per device
Your pinned tabs are stored locally on your device. If you log in on a different phone or clear the app data, you'll need to re-pin your preferred tabs.
