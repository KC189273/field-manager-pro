# Breaks

## How do I start a break?
1. You must be clocked in first.
2. Tap **Start Break** on the Clock screen.
3. Your break timer starts. Break time is deducted from your total hours.

## How do I end a break?
Tap **End Break** on the Clock screen. Your break duration is recorded.

## Can I take multiple breaks in one shift?
Yes. You can start and end as many breaks as needed during a single shift. Each break is tracked separately and all break time is deducted from your gross hours.

## Why does it say "Already on break"?
You already have an active break running. You need to end your current break before starting a new one.

## Why does it say "Not on break"?
You tried to end a break but you don't have one active. This can happen if the break was already ended, or if the app refreshed.

## Why does it say "No active shift"?
You tried to start a break but you're not clocked in. Clock in first, then start your break.

## Can my DM edit my breaks?
Yes. DMs (managers) and above can manually add or edit breaks on your shift. This is done from the Timecards page by tapping on your shift and editing the break times. Only managers, ops managers, SD, owners, and developers can do this — employees cannot edit their own breaks.

## How is break time calculated on my timecard?
Your timecard shows:
- **Gross Time**: total time from clock-in to clock-out
- **Break Deducted**: total break time (sum of all breaks)
- **Net Hours**: gross time minus break time — this is what counts for payroll

If you clock out while on break, the break is automatically ended at clock-out time.
