# Calendar & Resources

## Calendar

### Who can access the Calendar?
Managers, ops managers, SD, owners, and developers. Employees do not have access to the Calendar.

### How do I create a calendar event?
1. Go to **Calendar** from the nav.
2. Tap on a date or tap the **+** button.
3. Enter the event title, description, date/time, and optional location.
4. Optionally upload file attachments.
5. Set it as recurring if needed (daily, weekly, monthly) with optional exceptions.
6. Save. Participants receive notifications.

### Can I RSVP to an event?
Yes. Tap on any event to see its details and tap **RSVP**. The event creator can see who has responded.

### How do recurring events work?
When you create a recurring event, the system generates individual event instances on the recurring schedule. You can create exceptions for specific dates (e.g., skip a holiday).

### Can I attach files to events?
Yes. When creating or editing an event, you can upload attachments. Participants can download them from the event detail.

## Resources

### What is the Resources page?
A shared library for your organization containing documents, links, announcements, and contact information. It's organized into horizontal tabs.

### Who can view Resources?
All retail roles (employees, DMs, and above) can view the Resources page.

### Who can add or manage Resources?
Only **ops managers, sales directors, owners, and developers** can upload documents, add links, or manage content. DMs and employees have read-only access.

### What types of content can be shared?
- **Documents**: upload PDFs, Word docs, images, etc.
- **Links**: external URLs with descriptions
- **Announcements**: text posts visible to the team
- **Contacts**: team member contact cards with avatars
