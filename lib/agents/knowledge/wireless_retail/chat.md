# Chat / Messages

## Who can use chat?
Chat is available to **DMs (managers) and above** only. Employees do not have access to the chat feature.

## How do I start a conversation?
1. Go to **Messages** from the nav.
2. Tap the **+** button.
3. Choose **Direct Message** (one person) or **Group Chat** (multiple people).
4. For group chats, give the group a name and select members.
5. Start typing.

## How do I send photos or files?
Tap the **camera icon** to attach a photo, or the **paperclip icon** to attach a file. Photos are displayed inline in the chat. Files show as downloadable links.

## How do I send a GIF?
Tap the **GIF button** next to the message input. Search for a GIF using the search bar (powered by Tenor). Tap a GIF to send it.

## How do I react to a message?
**On mobile:** Long-press on a message to see the reaction picker. Choose from: 👍 ❤️ 😂 😮 🙌 ✅
**On desktop:** Right-click on a message.

Tap an existing reaction to see who reacted.

## How do I pin a message?
Long-press (or right-click) a message and select **Pin**. Pinned messages are accessible via the **bookmark icon** in the conversation header. Tap it to see all pinned messages.

## How do I add or remove members from a group chat?
1. Open the group chat.
2. Tap the **conversation name** at the top to open the info panel.
3. Tap **Members** to expand the member list.
4. To add: tap **Add Member**, search by name, and tap the + icon.
5. To remove: tap the minus icon next to a member's name.

System messages ("X added Y", "X removed Y") appear in the chat when members change.

## How do I mute a conversation?
Open the conversation, tap the conversation name at the top, and toggle the **Notifications** switch. When muted, you won't receive push notifications for new messages in that conversation, but you'll still see unread counts.

## Can I reply to a specific message?
Yes. Long-press (or right-click) a message and select **Reply**. Your reply will show the original message as a quote above your response.

## Can I mention someone?
Type **@** in the message input to see a list of participants you can mention. Select a name to mention them.

**@mentions DO trigger push notifications** — even if the mentioned person has the conversation muted. The push says "[Your Name] mentioned you: [message]."

You can also type **@everyone** to notify all participants in the conversation.
