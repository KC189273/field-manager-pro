# Expenses

## Who can submit expenses?
All roles except employees. DMs (managers), ops managers, sales directors, owners, and developers can submit expenses.

## How do I submit an expense?
1. Go to **Expenses** from the nav.
2. Tap **New Expense**.
3. Upload your receipt photo.
4. The **AI receipt scanner** (powered by Claude) will automatically extract the vendor, amount, and date from the receipt.
5. Review and correct any auto-filled fields.
6. Add a description/category.
7. Submit.

## Who approves expenses?
Sales Directors, Owners, and Developers can approve expenses. DMs and ops managers can submit but cannot approve.

## How do I approve an expense? (SD/Owner)
1. Go to Expenses.
2. You'll see pending expenses at the top.
3. Tap an expense to review the receipt and details.
4. Tap **Approve** or **Deny**.

## Where can I see my expense history?
All your submitted expenses appear on the Expenses page with their status (pending, approved, denied).

## Why can't I submit an expense?
Only managers and above can submit expenses. If you're an employee, ask your DM to submit the expense on your behalf.
