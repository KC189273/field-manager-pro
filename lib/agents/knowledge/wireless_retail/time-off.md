# Time Off Requests

## How do I request time off?
1. Go to **Time Off** from the nav.
2. Select your start date and end date.
3. For partial-day requests, also set start time and end time.
4. Submit the request. Your DM is notified and will approve or deny it.

## Why does it say I'm already scheduled?
The exact error: **"You are already scheduled on one or more of the requested dates. Please use Shift Swaps to find coverage instead."**

This happens when:
1. You have a shift on the requested date(s) in the published schedule.
2. The week containing that date has been published by your DM.

You cannot request time off for dates where you're already on a published schedule. Use **Shift Swaps** to find someone to cover your shift instead.

If the schedule hasn't been published yet, you CAN request time off for those dates.

## Can I edit a pending request?
Yes, but only while it's still pending. Go to Time Off, find your request, and tap Edit. You can change the dates. Once it's been approved or denied, it cannot be edited.

## Can I cancel a request?
You can cancel a pending or approved request. Go to Time Off, find the request, and tap Cancel. Denied requests cannot be cancelled.

## Who approves time off?
Your DM (manager) approves or denies requests. If no manager is assigned to your account, you'll see the error: **"No approver found. Please contact your manager."**

## Is there a PTO vs Sick type selector?
No. Time off requests have a start date, end date, and an optional reason field (free text). There is no separate PTO vs Sick type dropdown. If you need to specify the type, note it in the reason field.
