# Troubleshooting — Wireless Retail

## Clock In / Clock Out

**"Already clocked in"**
You have an active shift. Go to Clock and tap Clock Out before trying to clock in again.

**Clock-in button is grayed out or unresponsive**
You may already be clocked in on another shift. Check your current status on the Clock page. If you're sure you're not clocked in, try force-closing the app and reopening it.

**"No active shift" when trying to clock out**
Your shift may have been auto-clocked out by the system (runs at 9 PM CST daily) or manually ended by your DM. Check your timecards to see if the shift shows as completed.

**GPS location not recording**
Enable Location Services for Field Manager Pro in your phone settings. Set it to "Always" (iOS) or "Allow all the time" (Android). The clock-in still works without GPS, but your location won't be recorded.

## Breaks

**"Already on break"**
You have an active break running. Tap End Break first.

**"Not on break"**
You tried to end a break that's already ended or wasn't started. This can happen if the page refreshed.

**"No active shift" when trying to start break**
You need to be clocked in before you can start a break.

## Scheduling

**Can't see my schedule**
Your DM hasn't published the schedule yet. Ask your DM to publish it from the Store Schedule page.

**Wrong store or time on my schedule**
Contact your DM. Only DMs can edit the published schedule.

## Shift Swaps

**"Can only swap with employees under the same manager"**
You and the person you want to swap with must both report to the same DM. Cross-DM swaps are not supported.

**"One of these shifts already has a pending swap request"**
One of the shifts you selected is already part of another pending swap. Wait for that swap to be resolved first.

**Can't create a swap request**
Only employees can create swap requests. DMs and above edit the schedule directly instead.

## Time Off

**"You are already scheduled on one or more of the requested dates. Please use Shift Swaps to find coverage instead."**
You have a published shift on the date(s) you're requesting off. You need to get your shift covered through Shift Swaps first, then request the time off.

**"No approver found. Please contact your manager."**
Your account doesn't have a manager assigned. Contact your DM or the SD to get your manager_id set.

## Timecards

**Can't edit my own timecard**
Employees cannot edit their own timecards. Contact your DM. DMs also cannot edit their own timecards — they need to contact the Sales Director.

**"Timecards for this period are locked"**
Your DM has already submitted timecards for this pay period. Only the Sales Director, Owner, or Developer can make edits after submission.

**Can't see a floater's timecard**
Timecards are filtered by manager assignment. If the floater is assigned to a different DM, you won't see their timecard. The SD needs to change the floater's manager on the Team page.

## Chat

**Can't see Messages / Chat**
Chat is only available to DMs (managers) and above. Employees do not have access.

## Map

**Can't see the Live Map**
The Live Map is only available to Sales Directors, Owners, and Developers. DMs and below do not have access.

## Expenses

**Can't submit an expense**
Only managers and above can submit expenses. Employees should ask their DM to submit on their behalf.

## Payroll

**"This pay period has not yet closed"**
You're trying to submit timecards for a pay period that hasn't ended yet. Wait until the period end date passes.

**DM can't edit own timecard for payroll**
DMs cannot adjust their own timecards. Contact the Sales Director.

## Supply Requests

**Supply request stuck in "pending"**
If nobody orders your supplies within 48 hours, the system auto-escalates to the next level. If it's been longer, contact your DM or ops manager directly.

## Facility Tickets

**Ticket won't submit**
Make sure you've uploaded a photo — it's required for all facility tickets. Also check that you've selected a store, category, title, and urgency level.

## General

**App shows blank/white screen**
1. Force-close the app and reopen.
2. Check your internet connection.
3. Clear your browser cache (if using web version).
4. Log out and log back in.

**Not receiving push notifications**
1. Check Settings > Notifications in the app — make sure notifications are enabled.
2. Check your phone's notification settings for Field Manager Pro.
3. On iOS, make sure notifications are allowed. On Android, check that the app isn't in battery optimization mode.

**Forgot my password**
Tap "Forgot Password" on the login screen. Enter your email. A reset link will be sent (expires after 1 hour).

**Can't log in**
- Usernames are case-sensitive. Make sure you're entering it exactly right.
- Use your username, not your email, to log in.
- If you've forgotten your password, use the Forgot Password link.
- If your account was deactivated, contact your DM.
