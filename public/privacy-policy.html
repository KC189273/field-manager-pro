Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

Overview

Field Manager Pro ("the App") is a field team management application used by businesses to manage employee schedules, time tracking, and field operations. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used and protected.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: Your full name, username, and email address, provided by your employer when your account is created.
  • Precise location data (GPS): Your device's GPS coordinates are collected while you are clocked in, including when the app is running in the background. This is used to verify your clock-in location and to record your movement during your work shift. Location tracking stops when you clock out.
  • Clock records: Clock-in and clock-out times and the GPS coordinates recorded at those events.
  • Form submissions: Data you enter into visit reports, checklists, task completions, and expense forms as part of your job duties.
  • Photos: Images you upload as part of visit reports, expense submissions, or facility requests.
  • Device push notification token: Used only to deliver work-related notifications (shift reminders, task assignments, approvals) to your device.

Location Data — Detailed Disclosure

Field Manager Pro collects precise GPS location data, including background location, while an active work shift is in progress.
  • What is collected: Latitude and longitude coordinates from your device's GPS.
  • When it is collected: Automatically at regular intervals while you are clocked in, including when the app is in the background or the screen is off.
  • Why it is collected: To verify your physical presence at assigned work locations, record your work route for your employer, and support time and attendance record-keeping.
  • Who can see it: Authorized managers and operations staff within your organization only. Your employer controls access within their organization.
  • When it stops: Location collection stops immediately when you clock out. No location data is collected outside of active shifts.
  • Third-party sharing: We do not sell or share your location data with advertisers or unrelated third parties. Location data may be processed by our infrastructure providers (cloud hosting, database) solely to operate the service.

Before your first clock-in, you will be shown a disclosure explaining that background location will be collected during shifts, and you will be asked to grant location permission. You may revoke location permission in your device settings at any time; however, doing so will prevent you from using the clock-in feature.

How We Use Your Information

  • To record and manage employee work shifts and time records for your employer
  • To provide managers with visibility into field team activity and location during working hours
  • To generate reports for payroll, operations, and compliance purposes
  • To send you work-related push notifications (shift reminders, task assignments, approvals)
  • To improve the reliability and performance of the App

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Your data is shared only:

  • Within your organization (between employees, managers, and administrators) as necessary to operate the service
  • With infrastructure providers (Vercel, Supabase, AWS S3, Apple Push Notification Service) who process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality and data protection obligations

Data Retention

Data is retained for as long as your account is active or as required by your organization. You may contact your organization's administrator to request data deletion.

Security

All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. Passwords are stored using strong one-way hashing. Access to data is restricted by role-based permissions.

Children's Privacy

The App is intended for use by employees aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact your organization's administrator or reach out to us directly.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@fieldmanagerpro.app.
